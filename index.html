Projects

Zipizi, Make your thesis easy.

Zipizi is a service to assist students incapable of making progress in their theses or final projects. Zipizi offers services like consulting, counseling, and mini-classes. We help students to decide their topic, find resources for their research, and write better. The project takes advantage of Wordpress practicality with custom codes in HTML, JavaScript, CSS, PHP

Scuderia, A machine learning model to predict Forex prices

Scuderia was a project I started with a couple of friends who were enthusiastic about the potential of machine learning to predict Forex prices. I provided the background knowledge, data, and together with my good friends Aditya and Leonardo discussed the architecture for the model

Bunglom, Finding Diamonds in the Rough

Bunglom is a sister idea to Zipizi. Still breathing the same spirit of bettering Indonesia's education, Bunglom focuses on Indonesian students in need of assistance and mentorship during contest participation. Bunglom is a project actively under development and will start to induct bright Indonesian students mid-February. The project takes advantage of Wordpress practicality with custom codes in HTML, JavaScript, CSS, PHP

Genius Tic-Tac-Toe, A machine that always wins

When I took Formal Language Theory and Automata, I learned finite states and was tasked to map out all the possible states for a game of Tic-Tac-Toe. I was given liberty on the choice of programming language and I chose Pascal to improve my versatility in using uncommon programming language.
